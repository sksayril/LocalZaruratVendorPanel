Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Local Zarurat ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our seller platform at seller.localzarurat.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address, PAN/GST details (for sellers).

  • Payment Information: Razorpay transaction IDs and billing details. (Note: We do not store card/bank details.)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage seller accounts.
  • To process payments and refunds through Razorpay.
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience.
  • To send important service or security updates.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your data with:

  • Razorpay for payment processing.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. All transactions are encrypted and secured using HTTPS protocols.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for session tracking and improving website performance. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us.
